Azoogi's AZ-NF360 Neon Flex 360 is engineered to redefine the limits of architectural lighting designs. Unlike conventional linear lights that direct output in a single plane, the NF360 projects light uniformly across a full 360-degree diameter. This continuous illumination profile hides all hotspot details and eliminates standard dark spaces completely.

Its unique chemical-grade silicone sleeve is highly UV-stabilized, salt-mist resistant, and IP67 protected, making it highly robust for high-end coastal facades, gardens, hospitality venues, and architectural statements alike. Combined with 3-Step MacAdam binning and a High Color Rendering Index (CRI >90), it delivers visual comfort and color accuracy suited for photography, dining, and luxury commercial settings.

Proper installation ensures longevity and keeps your warranty valid. Please follow these installation rules:

  • Do not twist the product: This product bends horizontally/vertically depending on profile chosen, but twisting the internal FPCB can fracture solder points.
  • Max Single Feed Run: Limit single feed power configurations to 10 meters. For runs between 10m and 15m, supply power from both ends in parallel loops to avoid luminance drop due to copper line resistance.
  • Appropriate Driver Placement: Position drivers in well-ventilated boxes. Keep the secondary DC wire runs under 10 meters to prevent DC voltage losses.
  • Mounting: Use Azoogi stainless steel mounting clips or continuous aluminum tracks. Space mounting clips every 300mm.
